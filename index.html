Topics We Cover

We cover a variety of topics. The main topics are listed here:

Math

Our tutors cover the majority of middle school and high school math. This includes but is not limited to: Algebra, Geometry, Precalculus, and Calculus.

Computer Science

Our tutors can tutor Java, which is covered in APCS A, and Python, a prominent language used in many applications of computer science.

Econ/Finance

Many of our tutors aspire to become econ/finance majors, and they can tutor you to on this subject.

Science

Our tutors have taken AP Physics, AP Chemistry, and AP Bio, and they can definitely help you!
