Us

How to gain volunteer hours:

There are two ways you can gain volunteer hours. The first way is to become a tutor. Our tutors earn volunteer hours for each session of tutoring, plus the amount of time the tutor puts into creating course material. Another way to gain colunteering hours is to upload pdfs or scanned images of notes/study guides.
